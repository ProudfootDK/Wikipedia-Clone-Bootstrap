Thomas Proudfoot (born May 16, 1997) is a Danish-American programmer and amateur writer. He is currently studying Information Science at Pennsylvania State University with an expected graduation in Fall 2024.

Early life and education

Thomas Proudfoot was born on May 16, 1997, in Wyomissing, Pennsylvania, to Mark and Hanne Proudfoot. He spent his formative years in Wyomissing, a small town known for its tight-knit community and scenic landscapes. Growing up, Proudfoot showed an early fascination with computers and technology, often spending hours exploring the intricacies of software and hardware.

Attending Wyomissing Area Junior/Senior High School, Proudfoot's passion for technology continued to flourish. He actively participated in the school's computer science club, where he honed his programming skills and collaborated with peers on various projects. Additionally, he excelled academically, earning recognition for his achievements in mathematics and science.

Upon graduating from high school, Proudfoot faced a pivotal decision regarding his future education. With a strong interest in technology and a desire to make a meaningful impact in the field, he chose to pursue a degree in Information Science at Pennsylvania State University (Penn State).

Transitioning to university life, Proudfoot found himself immersed in a vibrant academic environment, surrounded by like-minded individuals passionate about technology and innovation. The Information Science program at Penn State provided him with a comprehensive foundation in computer science, data analysis, and information technology.

Outside the classroom, Proudfoot continued to pursue his passion for technology through extracurricular activities and personal projects. He participated in hackathons, coding competitions, and club meetings, further expanding his skills and network within the industry.
